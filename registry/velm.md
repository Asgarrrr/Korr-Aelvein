# Velm

A single notebook for everything that comes next. Sketches, fragments, half-formed thoughts, threads to pull on later — whatever doesn't yet belong in `world.md`, `systems.md`, `vocabulary.md`, or `story.md`.

When something here crystallizes, it migrates to its proper file. Until then, it stays here, in whatever shape it took when first written down.

No structure imposed. No section headers required. Write freely; sort later.
